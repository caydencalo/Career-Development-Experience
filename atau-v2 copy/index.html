About

Alpha Theta Tau

Alpha Theta Tau was founded on November 3rd, 2025, when thirteen men came together at Washington State University in Pullman, Washington, to build something greater than themselves.

We have established the first chapter of Alpha Theta Tau with a mission of uplifting communities, championing men's mental health, and creating a lasting, positive environment for every generation.

We believe that brotherhood built on authenticity makes better men, better leaders, better advocates, and better members of the communities we serve. Alpha Theta Tau is not just a chapter — it is a movement, and it is only just beginning.

History

Where it began

It all started with five men — Kobe Bui, John Conlan, Nicolas DeJesus, Anthony Mackay, and Hank Corrion — who shared their own personal battles with mental health. Coming from different Asian-American backgrounds, they arrived at the same conclusion: that the experiences that had shaped them did not have to break the men who came after them.

They chose to turn their pain into purpose.

Philanthropy

To stand for men's mental health

To build the communities that make that possible. Every program, every event, every brotherhood bond is in service of this mission.

2025–2026

FA25 Founding Class Unveiling

On November 17th, the Alpha Theta Tau Founding Class was unveiled at Washington State University within the doors of the Todd Auditorium. Thirteen men unified together made their presence known within the MGC community, establishing the very first chapter of Alpha Theta Tau.

United with the mission of uplifting every community they touch, to champion men's mental health with courage and conviction, and to light a path so bright that every man who comes after them will never have to walk in the dark alone.

Alpha Theta Tau launched its very first fundraising event, led by Fundraising Chair Marshall Nguyen, and the response from the WSU community was nothing short of incredible. Push for Change

Join the ATAU Legacy Fund and become a founding force behind a movement that is just beginning. Your commitment goes beyond financial support — it is an investment in a brotherhood built on authenticity, compassion, and the courage to champion men's mental health at every level.

Every contribution directly fuels the programs, outreach, and initiatives that make our mission real. From mental health advocacy on campus to community events that unite and uplift, your generosity powers the work that sets Alpha Theta Tau apart.

When thirteen men gathered at Washington State University on November 3rd, 2025, they laid the foundation for something greater than themselves. The ATAU Legacy Fund honors that founding spirit by ensuring that every generation of brothers has the resources, opportunities, and platform to carry that mission forward — and to bring new chapters of Alpha Theta Tau to life across the country.

Give today and stand with us — not just as a donor, but as a cornerstone of the Alpha Theta Tau legacy.
